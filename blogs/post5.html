Day 5: Living in the Moment ⏳

November 2, 2025

I just saw a quote that gave me a feeling I had to share. It goes like this:

"The tension of the future and the regret of the past are just destroying the present."

This one touched me deep down. The words are so correct, right? We aren't really living in the present. We're either thinking about our future and worrying, or we're thinking about our past and feeling regret for the things we lost.

What I want to say is, live in the moment. You won't get the past back, and the future isn't entirely in your hands. You can choose your destination, but you need to work hard today to reach that destination tomorrow.

Well, that's it. I just wanted to share that quote. Peace. ✌️
